Simplifying Your Website Journey

FIRST STEPS:
Don't know where to start? This is the spot! If you're not sure where to look first, this is the section for you.

This section is meant to take the guess work out of aimlessly scrolling and clicking until you find where you need to be. Shopping online for services—especially websites or niche products—can be overwhelming, especially if you’re not sure what to look for or how to start. That's why we've made it easy. Below, you’ll find answers to common questions and guidance tailored to your needs. Whether you're a small business owner, an entrepreneur, or just someone with an idea, this section will help you navigate the process.

  • Are You a Business Looking for a Website?

    Starting fresh? We specialize in building custom websites that reflect your brand and meet your business goals. From design to deployment, we’ll guide you through every step. Plus, we offer a monthly maintenance plan to handle updates, edits, and ongoing upkeep, so your site stays fresh and functional without any hassle.

  • Flexible Payment Options for Your Website

    We understand every business has unique financial needs, so we offer two payment programs to make having a website easier. Choose an upfront lump sum with lower ongoing monthly fees, or opt for a lower upfront cost—or even no upfront cost—and a larger monthly fee. With either plan, your website stays with us, ensuring seamless updates, maintenance, and cohesion, so you can focus on growing your business while we handle the rest.

    • Option 1: Flat Fee + Monthly Retainer

      Initial Investment: $1,500 (one-time)
      Ongoing Maintenance: $25/month
      This plan is perfect for those looking for a high-quality website at a competitive upfront cost. It includes ongoing maintenance to ensure your website remains up-to-date, secure, and optimized.

    • Option 2: Monthly Subscription for Ongoing Growth

      Monthly Fee: $160 (with a 6-month commitment, paid upfront)
      A strong online presence takes time to build and refine. This plan is designed for clients who prefer a lower initial cost, with ongoing SEO and site improvements included. The 6-month commitment ensures we have the time and resources to create and fine-tune your site for long-term success.

  • Not Happy With Your Current Website?

    If your current website isn’t performing or doesn’t represent your business, let us take a look. We’ll analyze its functionality, design, and speed to recommend or implement improvements.

  • Unsure What Kind of Website You Need?

    Whether you need a simple 5-page static website, an extensive multi-page platform, an eCommerce store, or a custom portfolio or blog, we’ll help you figure out the best type of website for your needs—and make it happen. Need more than 5 pages or have unique requirements? Let’s talk!

  • Are You Looking to Improve Your Website’s Speed or SEO?

    We use Google PageSpeed Insights and other tools to optimize performance, ensuring fast load times and higher search engine rankings.

  • Need Help with Content or Updates?

    We offer services to create and update your website’s content so it always looks fresh and professional.

  • Don’t Have a Clear Vision Yet?

    No problem! Our consultation services will help you uncover your goals and determine what your ideal website should look like.
